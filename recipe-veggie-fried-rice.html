Veggie Fried Rice

Quick fried rice with mixed vegetables and day-old rice. Perfect for leftovers.

4.5 (320 Reviews)
| 25 min | Serves: 4 | 380 Calories
Watch Video
Veggie Fried Rice Recipe
3:05
Ingredients
  • ✓
    3 cups cooked day-old rice
  • ✓
    2 eggs, beaten (optional)
  • ✓
    1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • ✓
    3 green onions, sliced
  • ✓
    2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✓
    1 tbsp minced ginger (optional)
  • ✓
    2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • ✓
    1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • ✓
    1 tbsp sesame oil
  • ✓
    1 tbsp neutral oil
  • ✓
    Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • ✓
    Red pepper flakes (optional)
Instructions
  1. 1
    Heat neutral oil in a skillet over medium-high. Scramble eggs; remove and set aside.
  2. 2
    Sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant; add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  3. 3
    Add day-old rice; break up clumps and stir-fry 2–3 minutes.
  4. 4
    Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce; return eggs to pan and mix well.
  5. 5
    Finish with sesame oil and green onions; season to taste and serve.
Tips & Variations
  • Use leftover rotisserie chicken or tofu for extra protein.
  • Day-old rice gives the best texture; spread fresh rice to cool and dry.
  • Swap soy sauce for tamari to keep it gluten-free.
  • Add kimchi or chili crisp for heat and tang.
