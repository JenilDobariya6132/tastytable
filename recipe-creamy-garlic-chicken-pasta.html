Creamy Garlic Chicken Pasta

A rich, comforting pasta made with tender chicken and garlic cream sauce.

4.8 (259 Reviews)
| 30 min | Serves: 4 | 620 Calories
Watch Video
Creamy Garlic Chicken Pasta Recipe
1:58
Ingredients
  • ✓
    8 oz pasta (fettuccine or penne)
  • ✓
    2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • ✓
    4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✓
    2 tbsp olive oil
  • ✓
    2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • ✓
    1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • ✓
    1 cup heavy cream
  • ✓
    1 cup grated Parmesan
  • ✓
    1 tbsp lemon juice
  • ✓
    2 tbsp chopped parsley
Instructions
  1. 1
    Cook the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. 2
    Saute the chicken until golden brown.
  3. 3
    Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. 4
    Pour in the cream and Parmesan, simmer until thickened.
  5. 5
    Mix in the pasta and toss to coat. Serve hot.
Tips & Variations
  • Use shrimp instead of chicken.
  • For a lighter version use half-and-half.
  • Add spinach or sun-dried tomatoes.
