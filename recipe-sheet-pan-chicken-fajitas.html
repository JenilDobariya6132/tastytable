Sheet-Pan Chicken Fajitas

Quick sheet-pan fajitas with chicken, peppers, and onions—perfect for busy nights.

4.7 (180 Reviews)
| 30 min | Serves: 4 | 460 Calories
Watch Video
Sheet-Pan Chicken Fajitas Recipe
2:15
Ingredients
  • ✓
    1 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • ✓
    2 bell peppers, sliced
  • ✓
    1 large onion, sliced
  • ✓
    2 tbsp olive oil
  • ✓
    2 tsp chili powder
  • ✓
    1 tsp ground cumin
  • ✓
    1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ✓
    1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • ✓
    1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • ✓
    1/4 tsp cayenne (optional)
  • ✓
    1/2 tsp salt
  • ✓
    Black pepper, to taste
  • ✓
    8 small flour tortillas
  • ✓
    Lime wedges and chopped cilantro
  • ✓
    Optional toppings: sour cream, salsa, avocado
Instructions
  1. 1
    Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment.
  2. 2
    Toss chicken, peppers, and onion with olive oil and spices; season with salt and pepper.
  3. 3
    Spread on sheet pan and roast 18–22 minutes until chicken is cooked and vegetables are tender.
  4. 4
    Warm tortillas. Toss fajita mixture with lime juice and cilantro.
  5. 5
    Serve with desired toppings.
Tips & Variations
  • Use chicken thighs for extra juiciness.
  • Add mushrooms or zucchini to stretch the veggies.
  • Low-carb option: serve with lettuce wraps.
  • Double the spice mix and store for quick repeats.
